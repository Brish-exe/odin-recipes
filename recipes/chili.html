Ingredients
  • Italian Sausage (1 pound)
  • 2 cans of Red Kidney Beans
  • Ground Meat (1 pound)
  • Can of Tomato Paste
  • 2 cans of Tomato sauce (1 can with green chilis)
  • Garlic
Steps
  1. Grab all Ingredients
  2. Throw them in the air
  3. Chop the air with your hands (Intensity is recommended but not required)
  4. Tada! You have outstanding chili in a crockpot.
